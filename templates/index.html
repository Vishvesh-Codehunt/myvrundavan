Our Policies

Safety:
VRUNDAVAN CONSTRUCTION is committed to provide safe working condition to all its employees and other involved in its activities & members of the public affected by such activities. This shall be achieved by eliminating or minimizing the hazard potentials through the adoption of proper management of risk control & providing a better living and working conditions at the construction sites.

  • Ensure all employees understand their accountability towards Safety and demonstrate visible Safety leadership.
  • Comply fully with all relevant Safety legislation regulations, standards, guidance and codes of practice.
  • Provide and maintain safe plant, equipment, systems of work and work facilities for the welfare of employees.
  • Provide sufficient information, instruction, training and supervision for employees to enable them to carry out their tasks competently and safely.
  • Ensure raw materials, articles and wastes are correctly handled, used and stored to avoid damage to the employees or the environment.

Environment:
As industry's one of the leading provider of highly valued technical and management solutions, we are firmly committed to maintain a safe and healthy environment in all of our offices & in our every project. We ensure this policy of environment protection by:

  • Ensure raw materials, articles and wastes are correctly handled.
  • Replace materials which are hazardous to the environment.
  • Minimize production of wastes at source.
  • Provide sufficient information on how to handle environment-sensitive products.
  • Identify areas for potential improvements after regular intervals.
